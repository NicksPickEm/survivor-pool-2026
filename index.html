Core rules
One pick per day. Pick one team playing that day. They win — you survive. They lose — you're out. Each team can only be used once all tournament.
Plan your whole roadmap now. Don't just think about today. Map out which teams you'll use each round so you don't burn your best teams early.
Save 1-seeds for later. You need safe picks in the Sweet 16 and Elite Eight. Don't waste Duke or Michigan in Round 1 when Gonzaga will do the same job.
Go contrarian when it matters. If everyone in your pool picks Duke on the same day and Duke loses, you gain a massive edge by picking differently.
Never pick 8/9 matchups. Both teams are roughly 50/50. There are always better options on the same day.
